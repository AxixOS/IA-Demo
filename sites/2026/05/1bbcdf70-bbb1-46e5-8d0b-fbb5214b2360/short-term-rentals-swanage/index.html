What’s Included in Your Short-Term Stay

Enjoy a modern 2-bedroom top floor apartment overlooking Swanage Bay. The apartment features a private south-facing balcony, open-plan living space, lift access, and integrated kitchen appliances. You’re just a short walk from Swanage Beach, cafés, restaurants, and the Jurassic Coast. Private beach access is included for all guests.

  • 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom
  • Private balcony with sea views
  • Lift access to the apartment
  • WiFi and integrated kitchen appliances
  • Open-plan living and dining area
  • Short walk to Swanage Beach and town centre
  • Private access to the beach

Short-Term Rental FAQs

What amenities are included for short-term guests? +

The apartment includes two bedrooms, one bathroom, a private balcony with sea views, lift access, WiFi, an open-plan kitchen with integrated appliances, and private access to Swanage Beach.

How does the booking process work? +

You can book directly online with instant confirmation. Our local team is available to answer any questions before and during your stay.
