Strategic Assessment FAQ

What is a Strategic Assessment? +

A Strategic Assessment is a comprehensive evaluation of your organisation’s current position, identifying strengths, gaps, and opportunities. It uses your company and HR data to create a clear strategic score and action plan.

How does the Organisational Strategic Score work? +

The score is based on your responses to our 32-question Staff Score Test, which takes about 20 minutes. You receive immediate feedback and a detailed evaluation, forming the basis for your improvement roadmap.

How is WUJ’s approach different from traditional methods? +

We use agile sprints to deliver results in 8–12 weeks, focusing on actionable outcomes. Traditional assessments often take months and may not provide tailored solutions for your business.

What are common gaps uncovered in assessments? +

Typical gaps include unclear strategic priorities, lack of focus on key talent, and insufficient planning for future business needs. Our assessment highlights these and provides practical steps to address them.

How much does a Strategic Assessment cost? +

Costs vary depending on your organisation’s size and needs. Contact us for a tailored quote and to discuss your specific requirements.
