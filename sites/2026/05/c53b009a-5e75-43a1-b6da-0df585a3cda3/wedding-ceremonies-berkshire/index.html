Wedding Ceremony FAQs for Berkshire Couples

What types of wedding ceremonies do you offer in Berkshire? +

We offer traditional, modern, religious, non-religious, and blended ceremonies. Each ceremony is personalised to your wishes and can be held at your chosen venue in Berkshire.

How is a celebrant wedding different from a registrar wedding? +

A celebrant wedding is fully personalised, with every element crafted around your preferences. Unlike a registrar, a celebrant can include personal stories, rituals, and any religious or non-religious content you choose. Legal registration must be completed separately.

What is your process for planning a wedding ceremony? +

We start with a consultation to learn about you as a couple. We then work together to design your ceremony, write the script, and make revisions based on your feedback. On the day, we officiate and ensure everything runs smoothly.

What are your fees for wedding ceremonies in Berkshire? +

Our fees are per ceremony and include planning, writing, and officiating. Please contact us for a detailed quote and package options.

Can you recommend popular wedding venues in Berkshire? +

Yes, we have experience working at many Berkshire venues and can suggest options that suit your style and guest numbers. Let us know your preferences during our consultation.
