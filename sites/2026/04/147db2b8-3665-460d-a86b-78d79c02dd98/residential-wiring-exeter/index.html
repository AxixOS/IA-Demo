Residential Wiring FAQs

What does a full house rewiring involve? +

Full house rewiring replaces all outdated or unsafe electrical wiring in your home, ensuring compliance with current safety standards. This process typically includes updating sockets, switches, and the consumer unit.

How do I know if my home needs wiring upgrades? +

Signs include frequent blown fuses, flickering lights, or outlets that don’t work. If your property is over 30 years old and hasn’t been rewired, it’s wise to have an assessment.

Are your electricians certified? +

Yes, all our technicians are fully qualified and licensed to carry out residential wiring in Exeter.

How much does residential wiring cost? +

Costs depend on the size and complexity of the job. We offer free, transparent estimates with no hidden fees.

Can you help with smart home wiring? +

Absolutely. We install wiring for smart lighting, security systems, and other connected home features.
