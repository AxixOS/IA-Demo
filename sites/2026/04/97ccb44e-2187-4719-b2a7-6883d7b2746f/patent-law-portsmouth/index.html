Patent Law Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for obtaining a patent in the UK? +

The process starts with an eligibility check, followed by drafting and filing your application with the UK Intellectual Property Office. We guide you through examination, respond to office actions, and support you through to grant or appeal if necessary.

How do I know if my idea is patentable? +

We assess your invention against UK and European patent criteria during our initial consultation. Novelty, inventive step, and industrial applicability are key factors.

What are the costs and fee structures for patent law services in Portsmouth? +

Patent costs depend on complexity and scope. We provide clear, upfront guidance on fees, including application, prosecution, and any litigation costs.

What happens if my patent is challenged or infringed? +

We handle both patent litigation and settlements, advising you on the best course of action to protect your rights and resolve disputes efficiently.

How does your European Patent Office experience benefit me? +

Our direct experience at the European Patent Office means we understand the nuances of both UK and European patent systems, helping you avoid common pitfalls and strengthen your application.
