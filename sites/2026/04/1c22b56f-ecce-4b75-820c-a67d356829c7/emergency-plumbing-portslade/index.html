Emergency Plumbing FAQs

What qualifies as a plumbing emergency? +

A plumbing emergency is any situation where water is causing damage, there’s a risk of flooding, or your plumbing system has stopped working unexpectedly. Common emergencies include burst pipes, severe leaks, blocked drains, and boiler breakdowns.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call in Portslade? +

We aim to arrive within 1 hour of your call for emergencies in Portslade and the surrounding areas.

How much does emergency plumbing cost? +

We provide clear, upfront estimates before starting any work. Our pricing is transparent, with no hidden fees, so you know exactly what you’ll pay.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured? +

Yes, all our plumbers are fully licensed and insured for your peace of mind.

What should I do while waiting for the plumber to arrive? +

If safe, turn off the main water supply to prevent further damage. Avoid using affected fixtures and keep the area clear for when the plumber arrives.
